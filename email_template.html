Happy Tuesday everyone and welcome to the final CS Societies newsletter of the year.

We hope you've enjoyed everything that has been organized, all of the societies' committees work very hard to deliver these great events. We wish you all the very best with any exams you may have coming up and a fond farewell to anyone who is leaving us this summer.

Please do join us as we celebrate with this last week of events and we hope you'll get involved with an exciting new programme of activities in September. Remember, all events are open to all - no membership required!
